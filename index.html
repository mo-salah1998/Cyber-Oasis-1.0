Cyber Oasis 1.0

Hack the Dunes

The first Maghreb student forum & hackathon in cybersecurity.
Tunisia – Algeria – Libya, united to build a resilient cyber culture.
Three days of challenges, innovation, and regional cooperation.

A Journey Beyond the Digital Horizon

In the desert, finding an oasis is survival. In cyberspace, detecting a vulnerability is security.

Cyber Oasis 1.0 bridges this symbolism with one mission: to train the next generation of Maghreb cyber defenders.

Event Details

Faculty of Sciences of Gabès – Tunisia

October 24-26, 2025

3 Countries: Tunisia, Algeria, Libya

3 Days of Innovation

Program

Cyber Oasis 1.0 unfolds in three stages:

Before the Event

Online training sessions with international experts and student ambassadors.

During the Event

Inspiring conferences, hands-on workshops, CTF hackathon, networking, partnership agreements.

After

A lasting legacy – a cyclical event hosted each year by a Maghreb university.

Hack the Dunes – Capture the Flag

A Jeopardy-style CTF where the brightest student teams take on real cyber threats.

24 hours of adrenaline, strategy, and creativity
Prizes, recognition, and a place in the history of Maghreb cybersecurity
Teams of 2 students from Tunisia, Algeria, and Libya

Registration Requirements

  • Each team consists of 2 students (binôme)
  • Students from Tunisia, Algeria, or Libya
  • All levels welcome: Beginner to Advanced
  • Complete registration form required
